    TropicalSemiring.Trop — Type.
    Trop{MM<:MinMax, T<:Real}

    A Trop number is an element of a the tropical semi-ring. These come in two flavours Max and Min. In the Max case this is the semi-ring $(ℝ ∪ {-∞}, ⊕, ⊙)$ where $⊕$ is the usual multiplication and $⊙$ is the usual maximum. In the Min case this is the semi-ring $(ℝ ∪ {∞}, ⊕, ⊙)$ where $⊕$ is the usual multiplication and $⊙$ is the usual minimum.
