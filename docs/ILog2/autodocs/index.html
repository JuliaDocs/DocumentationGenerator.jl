    ILog2.ilog2 — Method.
    ilog2(n::Real)

    Compute the largest m such that 2^m <= n.

    Note

    float(2^53 - 1) is the largest n::Float64 for which eps(n) ≤ 1. For larger n::Float64, "the largest m such that 2^m <= n" is ambiguous, although ilog2 may return a number. For large enough n::Float64, ilog2 will throw an InexactError. These cautionary statements do not apply for n::Integer.
