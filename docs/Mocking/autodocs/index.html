    Mocking.Bindings — Method.
    Bindings(expr)

    Takes a function signature expression and extracts all of the bindings into either internal (defined by the signature) or external (defined outside of the signature).

    julia> Bindings(:(f{T<:Integer}(a::T, b::Base.Real=a, c=z)))
Mocking.Bindings(Set(Any[:c,:T,:a,:b,:f]),Set(Any[:z,:(Base.Real),:Integer]))
